## Scope

N^3 contains the Trinity Probability Well package and its Raphael support/crosswalk layer. It is a standalone academic tool and demonstrator that can later be connected back into the broader private Raphael codebase.

It does **not** rewrite, repair, replace, or re-derive the private Raphael Equations.

## Current proof-safe boundary

- TPW finite probability core: mathematically proven within the finite model.
- `(7,7,7)`: unique three-variable product-probability mode.
- `(7,...,7)`: unique finite N-variable product-measure mode for independent 2d6 sum variables.
- Continuous trajectory bridge: valid piecewise-linear simplex interpolation.
- Physical Raphael correspondence: support/crosswalk/falsification layer only; audit-pending where physical interpretation is involved.
