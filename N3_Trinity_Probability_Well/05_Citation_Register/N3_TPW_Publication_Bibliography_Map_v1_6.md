## Citation policy for publication

- Do not cite literature as proving Raphael physical unification.
- Literature supports standard mathematics only: probability, entropy, simplex/compositional geometry, group actions, dimensional audit method, and testing discipline.
- Raphael-side claims require their own private equation-source traceability and cannot inherit proof status from TPW mathematics.
