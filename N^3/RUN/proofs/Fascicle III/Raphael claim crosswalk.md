## Non-negotiable distinction

A GeoMatrix can act as a proof instrument, validation instrument, falsification instrument, or visual/formulaic support tool. This does not mean every domain-specific Raphael claim is externally proven by the dice model alone. Fascicle III strengthens claims by decomposing them into proven components and exposing empirical routes.

## Domain escalation rule

A domain claim can move upward only when it passes:

1. closure admissibility,
2. geometry admissibility,
3. transport/observer admissibility where relevant,
4. falsification definition,
5. evidence or expert validation for empirical classification.
