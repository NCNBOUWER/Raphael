# Fidelity Rating

## Rating scale

| Rating | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 0–99% | Incomplete or partially controlled. |
| 100% | Mathematically proven inside the stated model. |
| 110% | Mathematically proven and independently corroborated by at least two internal proof/check pathways. |
| 200% | Empirically validated by external data or measurement. |
| 250% | Empirically validated by two independent empirical methods with proof structure retained. |
