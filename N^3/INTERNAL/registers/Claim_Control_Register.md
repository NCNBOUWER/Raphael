# Claim Control Register

## Allowed claims

- TPW finite enumeration is complete for the stated finite model.
- The product probability is normalized.
- `(7,7,7)` is the unique three-variable product-probability mode.
- `(7,...,7)` is the unique finite N-variable product-measure mode under stated assumptions.
- Barycentric/simplex mapping is a valid normalized geometry layer.
- Gamma interpolation is a valid mathematical bridge from discrete states to continuous piecewise-linear paths.

## Conditional claims

- Raphael support, crosswalk, or falsification language is allowed only when framed as correspondence or audit support.
- Fidelity ratings above 100% refer to internal proof corroboration, not empirical physical certainty.

## Blocked claims

- TPW proves Theory of Everything status.
- TPW proves quantum gravity.
- TPW proves physical gravity equivalence.
- TPW proves hydrodynamics or Navier-Stokes.
- TPW proves mechanical N-body physics.
- TPW rewrites private Raphael equations.
